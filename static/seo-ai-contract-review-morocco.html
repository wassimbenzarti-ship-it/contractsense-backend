What is AI contract review
for Morocco?

AI contract review is the automated analysis of a contract's clauses against applicable law — in this case Moroccan law (Code des Obligations et Contrats, Labour Code, Commercial Code) and international standards. The AI identifies which clauses are unfavourable to your party, what legal protections are missing, and what exact text to add or modify. Omniscient does this in 60 seconds and exports the result as a Word file with Track Changes.
