What Is AI Contract Review? / Qu'est-ce que la revue de contrat par IA ?

EN AI contract review is the use of artificial intelligence — specifically large language models trained on legal corpora — to read, analyse, and flag issues in contractual documents. Rather than replacing lawyers, AI contract review dramatically accelerates the first-pass review, catching obvious risks, non-standard clauses, and deviations from applicable law before a qualified attorney performs a deeper analysis.

FR La revue de contrat par intelligence artificielle consiste à utiliser des modèles de langage de grande taille, entraînés sur des corpus juridiques, pour lire, analyser et signaler les problèmes dans les documents contractuels. Loin de remplacer les avocats, l'IA accélère considérablement la première lecture, en détectant les risques évidents, les clauses non-standard et les écarts par rapport au droit applicable, avant qu'un juriste qualifié n'effectue une analyse approfondie.

How Omniscient Works for Morocco Law

1. Upload Your Contract

Omniscient accepts PDF and DOCX files up to 50 pages. Your document is parsed paragraph by paragraph, preserving the structure of the original contract. Supported languages: French, Arabic (transliteration), English.

2. Jurisdiction Detection

Omniscient automatically identifies the governing law clause. For Moroccan contracts, the system activates its Moroccan law knowledge base covering the Code des Obligations et Contrats (D.O.C.), the Code du Travail (Loi 65-99), the Code de Commerce, and relevant Dahirs. For mixed jurisdictions — for example a Moroccan company contracting with a French partner — both legal frameworks are consulted simultaneously.

3. Hybrid RAG Analysis

The core of Omniscient is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) engine trained on thousands of annotated contract clauses, court decisions, and legal doctrine specific to Morocco, France, and the UK. When your contract is uploaded, the system performs:

  • Semantic vector search — finds the most legally relevant passages using Voyage AI embeddings (1024 dimensions)
  • BM25 keyword search — ensures important legal terms are never missed
  • Jurisdiction boost — sources matching your contract's jurisdiction are ranked higher

4. Clause-by-Clause Analysis

Every paragraph is evaluated for: compliance with mandatory Moroccan law provisions, fairness (one-sided clauses, imbalanced liability caps), completeness (missing mandatory clauses such as notice periods, confidentiality, governing law), and commercial risk (penalty clauses, automatic renewal, unilateral modification rights).

5. Actionable Suggestions

Omniscient does not simply flag problems — it suggests improved wording, aligned with both legal requirements and negotiation best practices. Each suggestion is rated by severity: critique (legal non-compliance), risque élevé (commercial risk), amélioration (best practice).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Omniscient a replacement for a lawyer?

No. Omniscient is a legal technology tool designed to assist lawyers and legally sophisticated users. It is not legal advice and does not replace the judgment of a qualified attorney. Westfield Avocats recommends using Omniscient as a first-pass tool before consulting a lawyer for significant contracts.

Which contracts can Omniscient analyse?

Employment contracts (contrats de travail), NDA / confidentiality agreements, service agreements, commercial contracts, partnership agreements, SaaS and software licences, and purchase agreements. Support for more contract types is added regularly.

Is my contract data secure?

Contracts are processed in isolated, encrypted sessions. Westfield Avocats does not store or use your contract content for model training. All data handling complies with applicable data protection regulations including Moroccan Law 09-08.

What languages are supported?

French is the primary language for Moroccan law analysis. English is fully supported for UK and international contracts. Mixed French/English contracts are handled natively.
